Passkeys let you sign in without checking your email. They're stored on your device and work with your fingerprint, face, or PIN — the same way you unlock your phone.

## Why use a passkey?

When you sign in with email, we send you a link and you wait for it to arrive. A passkey skips that step entirely. You tap the sign-in button, your device confirms it's you, and you're in.

## How to set one up

1. Sign in to your account
2. Go to [Settings](/admin/settings)
3. In the Passkeys section, click **Add a passkey**
4. Your browser will ask you to confirm — follow the prompts

You can add passkeys on multiple devices. Each one works independently.

## What if I lose my device?

You can always sign in with your email — passkeys are an extra convenience, not a replacement. If you lose a device, sign in with email and remove the old passkey from Settings.

## Learn more

Passkeys are an open standard supported by all major browsers and operating systems. For a deeper look at how they work, visit [passkeys.dev](https://passkeys.dev).
